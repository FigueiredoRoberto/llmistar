"A Variation Point is an explicit engineering decision that allows for multiple alternative Variants to be chosen during system development, based on specific Assets."

"In some systems, the selection of one variation point can lead to dependencies on other variation points. For instance, choosing a movie player might require selecting an audio player as well."

"Means-end links represent the relationship between an 'end' such as a goal, resource, or satisficed goal and alternative 'means' to achieve it, typically expressed as tasks. In a notation, an arrow points from the means to the end."
